BHUTAN TRAVEL NOW

The Art of Creative Tourism

15 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE… Entering a New Chapter

With a rich 15-year history in the tourism industry, I am thrilled to introduce you to my new venture:Bhutan Travel Now.

Back in 2008, when the annual tourist count stood at 28,000, I embarked on my journey as a tour guide, starting from the ground up. Fueled by my passion for showcasing Bhutan's unique culture and traditions to a discerning group of global travelers, I committed myself to continuous learning, staying ahead in my evolving expertise, and fully immersing in the "Art of Tourism."

Over the years, I have had the pleasure of hosting an eclectic mix of travelers—from those who invested years of savings to experience Bhutan to Royals seeking the peace and beauty of my country. This diverse spectrum provided invaluable insights into the world beyond our borders. In the subsequent 13 years, I nurtured a family business, leveraging my skills to attract a clientele who shared the belief that "travel is a way to enrich one's life." Even as I occasionally continue guiding, the experience has instilled in me a keen attention to detail and a commitment to personalized service.

Now, as I embark on a new chapter, it's time to infuse Bhutan's tourism industry with a fresh perspective. I am eager to bring my own ideas to life and add a new 'touch' to the journey ahead!

ABOUT THE TEAM OF ‘BHUTAN TRAVEL NOW’

Meet the incredible team at Bhutan Travel dedicated to enhancing your Bhutanese experience. With my expertise in navigating Bhutan's travel scene, I bring an insider's guide to the finest spots and hidden gems tailored to your tastes. We're not just colleagues; we're hospitality professionals with a deep local insight. When you visit Bhutan with us, it's more than a vacation—it's a chance to immerse yourself in a world where time slows down, and happiness is 'WithIn,' not 'Without.' Our team shares a love for details and impeccable customer service, fueled by a blend of professionalism and genuine passion. It's not just about travel business; it's about inspiring you to delve deeper, ask questions, and uncover what makes Bhutan truly special.
