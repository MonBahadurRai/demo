ADVENTURES ON TWO WHE E L S

From ‘Easy-going biking to the ‘Art of Mountain Trail-Riding’ to the “Himalayan Motorbike Riding Skills’: No better way to explore a destination than on two wheels. Cycling & Biking in general is like a feast for your eyes on Bhutan’s country roads offering the most spectacular sceneries on almost traffic-free streets.

If you're into the easy vibe, hit up the towns and villages on 100% tarmac roads where you stop at little towns or villages.If you're more of a mountain biking pro , get ready for some ups and downs on Bhutan's highest passes. Picture this: hilly landscapes, Buddhist prayer flags waving in the Himalayan breeze – a reminder that you are blessed. And for the die-hard motorbike fans, exploring The Land of the Thunder Dragon is basically living the dream on Earth. Oh, by the way, I'm a two-wheel fanatic so whether you're up for a chill family ride or some crazy mountain biking action, I've got all the info you need for your two-wheeled adventure. No question too simple or too wild!
